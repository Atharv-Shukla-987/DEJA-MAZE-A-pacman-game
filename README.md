# DEJA MAZE

A game passes on classic game pac -man but something creepy as there are no monsters to hunt but your own shadow of past trying to hunt you


# core mechanisms
 you spawn in a randomly genertaed room every time you start 
 you have to collect 80% of orbs in the maze to cross the level 
 but your own shadow is hunting you down 
 you havr to be calculative with every move you make

 as mazes are randomly generated so sometimes it may be a little tricky and may be impossible to pass a level

 # controls

 As usaual cclassic WASD for movement
